# 🥧 THE PEANUT BUTTER PIE OVERRIDE PROTOCOL
## [BUFFER: 🍨 🥧 🍦 🍡 🍧 🍩] -> [STABILIZER: ENGAGED]

### 🛒 INGREDIENTS
* **Base Registry:** 1 Deep-Dish Graham Cracker or Chocolate Cookie Crust
* **Core Density:** 1½ cups Creamy Peanut Butter (High-viscosity structural support)
* **Emulsion Layer:** 1 package (8 oz) Cream Cheese, softened
* **Decorative Logic:** 1 cup Powdered Sugar
* **Cloud Buffer:** 1 tub (8 oz) Whipped Topping, gently folded

### 🍳 CONFECTIONARY INSTRUCTIONS
1. **Initialize the Cream Core:** Combine cream cheese, peanut butter, and powdered sugar. Beat at a steady, rhythmic tempo until smooth.
2. **Fold the Cloud Buffer:** Gently introduce the whipped topping using a Systemic Fold (rotating from bottom up).
3. **Deploy to the Crust Layer:** Spoon the filling into the crust and smooth out the topography.
4. **Cryogenic Solidification:** Freeze for 1–2 hours until completely heavy, chilled, and unshakeable.
